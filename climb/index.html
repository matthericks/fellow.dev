Work Ethic 5.12a

Had hopped on a couple moves during my first time at Barn Bluff at least 10 years back when I was just starting climbing. It felt impossible.

Was on top rope for my first real attempt at it, and nearly flashed it. EVerything I had sorted out for beta felt great, and the holds that felt impossible before felt more like resting spots. The only move I didn't get on my first go I was able do after finishing the rest of the climb. It was pretty much what I had been doing, just trying a bit harder, and should go on my next attempt. In the moment it felt like pulling the move would have spent me for the rest of the climb, but after that single move the climb is more or less done and the rest is just a victory lap.
